C# Console Calculator

On my Github, you will find a repo for a C# console calculator that I wrote. The program itself is pretty straight-forward and doesn't allow for super advanced math, but it saves all your inputs to an array so you are able to choose however many numbers you need for your calculations. It also includes a FizzBuzz functionality, where it prints the numbers 1 through 100 and lets you know whether or not the number is divisible by three, five, or both three and five. I added this because it shows that I can use logic and get the intended result.
